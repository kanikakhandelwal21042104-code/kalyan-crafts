Introduction

Kalyan Crafts was established as an initiative of Samajik Yuva Sangthan Sansthan (SYSS) which works for women empowerment and rural livelihood development. The aim is to provide rural women with skill training and sustainable income opportunities.

This initiative is supported by Shree Balram Go Sewa Sadan, God Ka Bas Gaushala where women are trained to create eco-friendly cow dung based products along with handicrafts.

About Kalyan Crafts

Kalyan Crafts is an initiative of Samajik Yuva Sangathan Sansthan (SYSS), working for women empowerment and rural livelihood development.

The aim is to provide rural women with skill training and sustainable income opportunities.

This initiative is supported by Shree Balram Go Sewa Sadan Gaushala, where eco-friendly cow dung based products are created along with handicrafts.

Mission

  • To make rural women self-reliant and economically independent.
  • To promote traditional skills and handicrafts with a modern touch.
  • To encourage eco-friendly products from cow dung.
  • To create sustainable livelihood for women artisans.

Vision

Every rural woman should become empowered, respected and self-reliant through her own skills and creativity.

Focus Areas

  • Skill Development & Training
  • Product Innovation & Packaging
  • Market Linkage through exhibitions and e-commerce
  • Sustainable livelihood for rural women
